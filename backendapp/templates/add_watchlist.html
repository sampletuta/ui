Image Quality Guidelines
Recommended:
  • High resolution images (at least 200x200 pixels)
  • Clear, well-lit faces
  • Front-facing or slightly angled faces
  • Good contrast and focus
  • Natural lighting (avoid heavy filters)
Avoid:
  • Very small faces (below 100x100 pixels)
  • Blurry or low-quality images
  • Heavily filtered or edited photos
  • Faces in shadow or poor lighting
  • Side profiles or obscured faces
Note: Images with faces smaller than 20x20 pixels cannot be processed by the face recognition system.
